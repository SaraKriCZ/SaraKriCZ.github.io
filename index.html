✦ Closed species ✦

Meet the Jellies 🫧

My very own species — squishy, unique, and full of personality!

🌿 Who are Jellies?

Jellies are a closed species where personality and AU are completely flexible — your imagination is the only limit! Their jelly parts work like a lava lamp: they glow and have moving bubbles. Any animal or fantasy species can be a Jelli, with any body type, age, or height welcomed!

🔍 How to spot a Jelli?

🔱

Horns

Always 2+ horns — from giant to tiny, but always made from jelly!

✨

Jelly Spots

Small jelly spots on the face — can be any shape!

🫧

Jelly Tail

All Jellies have a jelly tail — sometimes subtle, but always there!

💎

Jelly Parts

Any part of the body can be jelly — any color, even multiple!

👁️

Eyes

Eyes always match the jelly color!

🐾

Species

Deer? Fish? Dragon? Any species can be a Jelli!

Jellies × UniAlies

Jellies

Closed species — available to buy
  • 2 or more horns
  • Any animal or fantasy species
  • Any part can be jelly
  • Any type of eyes
  • Gems on the face
  • Appearance is gender-neutral

UniAlies

Private species — not available
  • Only 1 horn
  • One specific species only
  • Only specific parts are jelly
  • One type of eyes
  • Antennas
  • Males and females differ in appearance

⚠️ Jellies are NOT UniAlies! They are two completely separate species!

Terms of Service

General & Usage

▼
Right to Refuse: I reserve the right to decline any commission for any reason — uncomfortable subject matter, rude behavior, or style mismatch.
Personal Use Only: Commissions are for personal use (PFPs, wallpapers, home prints). You may not sell or profit from the artwork.
Commercial Use: Streaming assets, merch, branding? Contact me to discuss the commercial fee!
NO AI Training: My artwork is strictly prohibited from being used for AI training, generation, or machine learning datasets of any kind.
Modifications: No editing, tracing, or altering my artwork without permission.
Copyright: I retain full copyright. I may post the finished commission on my social media and portfolio.
💳

Payment

▼
Method: Payments are made via PayPal in CZK currency.
Timing: Payment is required after the sketch is approved. Lining/coloring will not begin until payment is received.
Payment Plans: Split payments available only for orders over €100. Schedule agreed upon individually.
🎨

Process, Revisions & WIPs

▼
Workflow: I'll send frequent WIP updates so you can make sure the art is heading in the right direction!
Trello: Track your commission status on my Trello board.
Sketch Phase: Major changes (pose, composition, design) are free!
Coloring Phase: Only minor color tweaks allowed at this stage.
Finished Piece: No edits possible once done, or subject to an additional fee.

Refunds & Cancellations

Before payment/work starts: No charge, free to cancel!
