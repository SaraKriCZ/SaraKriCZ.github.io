✦ Closed species ✦

Meet the Jellies 🫧

My very own species — squishy, glowy, and full of personality!

🌿 What are Jellies?

Jellies are a closed species where personality and AU are completely flexible — your imagination is the only limit! Their jelly parts work like a lava lamp: they glow and have moving bubbles. Any animal or fantasy species can be a Jelli, with any body type, age, or height welcomed!

🔍 How to spot a Jelli?

🔱

Horns

Always 2+ horns — from giant to tiny, but always made from jelly!

✨

Jelly Spots

Small jelly spots on the face — can be any shape!

🫧

Jelly Tail

All Jellies have a jelly tail — sometimes subtle, but always there!

💎

Jelly Parts

Any part of the body can be jelly — any color, even multiple!

👁️

Eyes

Eyes always match the jelly color — no exceptions!

🐾

Species

Deer? Fish? Dragon? Any species can be a Jelli!

Jellies × UniAlies

Jellies

Closed species — available to buy
  • 2 or more horns
  • Any animal or fantasy species
  • Any part can be jelly
  • Any type of eyes
  • Gems on the face
  • Appearance is gender-neutral

UniAlies

Private species — not available
  • Only 1 horn
  • One specific species only
  • Only specific parts are jelly
  • One type of eyes
  • Antennas
  • Males and females differ in appearance

⚠️ Jellies are NOT UniAlies! They are separate species!
